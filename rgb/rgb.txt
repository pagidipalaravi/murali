What is rgb?
	RGB is stands for red, green, blue colors
	RGB having parameters 0 to 255.
	rgb(255,255,255)-It is shows color like "white".
	rgb(0,0,0)-It is shows color like "black".
	rgba-"a" is a alpha channel(opacity).
	alpha parameter are 0 to 0.1
	HSL-stands for Hue, saturation, lightness.
	HSLA-stands for Hue,Saturation,Lightness with alpha channel opacity.
